Admin Guidance

  • ID Integrity: University IDs are immutable once assigned to prevent academic record mismatch.

  • Email Standards: Ensure the email follows the @ul.edu.lb format for official portal access.

  • Major Validity: Changing a major may affect the student's credit requirements.

  • Status Change: Setting status to 'SUSPENDED' will immediately revoke campus facility and LMS access.
